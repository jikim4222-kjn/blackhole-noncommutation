# **examples/holographic_projection.md**

## Holographic Projection as a Structural Instance of Non-Commutation

### Categories
- **C**: bulk configurations  
- **O**: boundary‑accessible observables

### Stabilization
Bulk equilibration $S$ is modeled as an idempotent process.

### Observation
Boundary projection:

$$
\Pi:\text{bulk data} \to \text{boundary observables}.
$$

### Observable stabilization
Boundary coarse‑graining $S_O$.

### Comparison
$$
\eta_X:\Pi(SX)\to S_O(\Pi X)
$$

### Non‑invertibility and islands
If $S$ preserves distinctions that $\Pi$ erases, then $\eta_X$ is non‑invertible.

A region $I$ where $\eta_X$ becomes invertible behaves as an **island**.
