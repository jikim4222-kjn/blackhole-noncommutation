## Classical Coarse-Graining as a Minimal Commutative/Non-Commutative Test

### Categories
- **C**: finite sets with deterministic maps  
- **O**: coarse‑grained partitions

### Stabilization
Define:

$$
S(X)=X\times\{0,1\}
$$

with the projection forgetting the second coordinate.  
This is idempotent.

### Observation
Let $\Pi$ collapse elements into equivalence classes:

$$
\Pi(X)=X/{\sim}.
$$

### Observable stabilization
Let $S_O$ refine observable classes.

### Comparison
$$
\eta_X:\Pi(SX)\to S_O(\Pi X)
$$

### Non‑invertibility
If stabilization distinguishes elements that $\Pi$ identifies, then $\eta_X$ is not invertible.
